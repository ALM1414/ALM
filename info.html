Info

Born in 1965 in Rimini (Italy), Lucia Masi is an artist and designer based in London since 2015. Her work focuses on the love for organic shapes and colors found in nature, from which she draws inspiration and used in her paintings, sculptures and drawings. With a keen eye for the perfect color on the canvas, her paintings are a majestic flow of hues, which accompanied by her unique amorphous shapes, produce an iconic style, recognisable anywhere without context. The same phylosophy carries onto her sculptures, by creating objects that suck you in from every angle, defying any sense of logic and space. Influenced by her daily life, hobbies and favourite artists like Georgia O'Keeffe and Dora Maurer, her art continues to evolve and diverse through the passing of time.
